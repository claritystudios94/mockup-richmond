About the practice

Providing expert dental care
to Sheffield since the 1960s.

Richmond Dental Care has been part of Sheffield S13 for over sixty years. What began as a single surgery has grown into one of the area's most established practices — six surgeries, eight dentists, and a support team of over twenty.

"Trusted by the same Sheffield families across multiple generations."

Both NHS and private care are available under one roof. Two of our senior dentists are NHS Foundation Trainers — a status the NHS grants only to practices that meet the highest clinical standards. BDA Good Practice membership has been held and independently assessed for over ten consecutive years.
